CREATING SEAMLESS TRAVELLING EXPERIENCES

This project is a collaboration between SkyTeam and the university. I worked alongside 4 other team members in this 4 months long project. The goal of this project is to implement navigation services to promote seamless travelling experiences for air travel passengers. We have carefully followed steps of the design process to identify the problem areas and points where passengers get lost in order to figure out where and how to implement the navigation services.
