SHELF CUSTOMIZER FOR COUPLES

This is my master's thesis project. I designed and developed a wall shelf customizing application (with Unity 5.0) as a fully functional prototype for user tests. I also planned and conducted 2 ideation workshops (with 6 designers) and 2 qualitative user researches at homes and workplaces of 18 participants. After that, I performed detailed analysis on data from interviews and observational videos through thematic analysis
