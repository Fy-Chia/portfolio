Easy Chopsticks

Every child has to go through tedious practices when learning chopsticks. What if we can make the learning process fun and leisurable instead? Easy Chopsticks is an interactive training chopsticks comes with a digital game to make practicing time a play time. This project was presented at the ACE (Advances in Computer Entertainment) Conference in 2014 and received the Creative Showcase Bronze Award.
